What are Kaggle Days Meetups ?

Kaggle Days Meetups are a series of events all over the world that aim to gather Kagglers and people interested in Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence around one city. Kaggle Days Meetup, Delhi NCR is on a mission to gathers Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence Enthusiasts and most importantly Kaggle fans.

Why Kaggle Days Meetups?

  • Meet Kaggle enthusiasts from your city, exchange experience and network.
  • These meetups aim to gather local Data Science communities and share knowledge.
  • Each Meetup has it’s difficulty level described, so that you could find the one that suits you best.
  • You don’t need to buy ticket to participate in Meetups. Just join us at Meetup platform and come to the event!

    • Goals behind organizing such a meetup ?

  • LEARN how to start on Kaggle through practice.
  • NETWORK with other people.
  • MEET Kaggle Grandmasters, Masters and Kaggle competition winners.
  • HAVE FUN at the meetings and make new friends!
  • LEARN tips and trick on how to win a Kaggle competition.<
  • IMPROVE your skills.

    • Who are our potential speakers ?

    Our speakers are Kaggle competition winners, Kaggle Experts, etc. or anybody else who can help others guide about how to proceed with Kaggle Platform.

    Who can attend this meetup ?

    Anyone with an interest to explore Machine Learning, Data Science along with participation in some real world competitions over Kaggle can attend. This meetup is scheduled keeping in mind the concerns of a beginner to start with Kaggle.
